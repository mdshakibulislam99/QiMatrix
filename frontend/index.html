What is Feng Shui Analysis?

Feng Shui is the ancient Chinese practice of harmonizing individuals with their surrounding environment. Our AI-powered platform analyzes your space using both traditional principles and modern data to provide personalized insights.

01

Outdoor Location Analysis

Evaluate your property's location using real geographic data—nearby water sources, mountain formations, building density, road networks, and green spaces. Our AI examines how these factors influence the natural energy flow around your home or office.

02

Indoor Room Analysis

Assess individual rooms by analyzing orientation, natural light, color schemes, furniture placement, and elemental balance. Receive specific recommendations for bedrooms, living rooms, offices, and other spaces to enhance comfort and productivity.

03

Five Elements Balance

Understand how Wood, Fire, Earth, Metal, and Water elements interact in your space. Our analysis identifies imbalances and suggests remedies through color, materials, and spatial arrangements to achieve harmony.

How It Works

1

Choose Analysis Type

Select between outdoor location analysis (for houses, buildings, properties) or indoor room analysis (for specific rooms like bedrooms, offices, living areas). Each analysis type uses different Feng Shui principles and data sources.

2

Input Your Data

For outdoor analysis, click your location on the interactive map or search for an address. For indoor analysis, fill in details about your room including size, orientation, colors, natural light, and existing elements. The more accurate your input, the better your results.

3

AI Analysis Process

Our machine learning algorithm, trained on thousands of Feng Shui principles, analyzes your data. For outdoor locations, we fetch real geographic information including water proximity, green spaces, and terrain. For indoor spaces, we evaluate element balance, spatial flow, and energy patterns.

4

Get Your Results

Receive a comprehensive Feng Shui score (0-100) with detailed breakdowns across multiple categories. View interactive charts showing your Five Elements balance and specific aspect scores. Most importantly, get personalized recommendations you can implement immediately to improve your space's energy.
